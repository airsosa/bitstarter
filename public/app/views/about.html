About

Investing in the futre of society by unlocking the talents of women and children in the society

Conzultrix Impact is a three-pronged effort to empower some of the most disadvantaged persons in society. The approach is to:

  1. Provide children with critical foundational skills, competencies, and mindsets by nurturing the natural creativity inherent in all children through computer programming and design classes. This will develop their critical thinking abilities as they grow to provide innovative solutions to various challenges in the society.

  2. Encourage and empower women to take up more active roles in science, technology, leadership, business, and design by exposing them to a relevant training and mentoring system.

  3. Equip teachers and trainers with the requisite tools and knowledge to effectively engage the minds of the children they interact with thereby creating a multiplier effect.

Our goal is to build a self reliant society by exposing our participants to computer and manufacturing skills so that our young minds can have the know how to develop the technologies that would drive economic development.

We want to develop that girl child that will design and print her own circuit boards, solder components onto the boards and write the embedded programme that would enable her devices decide whether to slide the gate open (or not) by first scanning the vehicle registration number and the face of the driver.

We hope to build vocational skills such as tool design so that a lad can design the die that would stamp the sheet metal body of the next made-in-Nigeria cars or the cutting tool that would be used to machine a robotic arm.

Ultimately we are looking at building an inclusive and technically sound society - with active participation of women - that can provide innovative solutions to adequately meet our needs locally. With your support we can empower that rural kid or woman and enhance local productivity.
